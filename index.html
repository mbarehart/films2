Big Fish

Big Fish is directed by Tim Burton. The movie rovolves around estranged father (Edward) and son (Will). Edward is dying from cancer and Will returns to his parents' home to spend time with his father. The father and son reconnect as Edward recounts stories from his life with as much exaggeration and fantasy as you can expect from a movie named Big Fish. Tim Burton's dazzling visuals keep you engaged throughout the fantasy filled flashbacks, and allow you to feel the heartfelt moments. Check out the trailer below.
