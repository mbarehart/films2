A Walk in the Woods

A Walk in the Woods stars RobertRed as Bill Bryson, an aging travel writer recently returnd to the USA. His wife (played by Emma Thompson) isn't to keen on the idea, citing his age as a major problem. She insists he does not go alone. Bill's old travel buddy Steven Katz (Nick Nolte) decides to join. They start at the Georgia end of the trai in April. Bonding and adventure ensue. You can see the trailer below.


There are some lovely mountain views along the hike. Along the hike, our characters see some lovely views and stop in some towns along the way for a rest. One of the places they see in their travels is Fontana Dam. Fontana Dam is the tallest dam in the eastern U.S. It is located in North Carolina just outside of the Tennessee border. The Appalachian Trail crosses over the top of the dam. I was lucky enought to vist the my first time when the flood gates were open. The video doesn't begin to capture the awe and power of the water spewing forth.
